How It Works

User-Managed System: You have full control over your bills and subscriptions.

  • Add each bill/subscription manually
  • Select the category (won't auto-detect food, coffee, etc.)
  • Set the amount, frequency, and due date
  • Bills appear on your calendar

AI Role: AI only warns you about upcoming payments based on what you've added.

Features
  • Calendar Integration: See bills on your calendar view
  • Smart Reminders: Get AI warnings before bills are due
  • Flexible Frequencies: Weekly, biweekly, monthly, quarterly, annual
  • Pause/Resume: Temporarily pause payments without deleting them
  • Cost Tracking: See total monthly and annual costs
