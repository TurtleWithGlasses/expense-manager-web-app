Step 1 Creating Categories

Categories help you organize your income and expenses. Before adding any entries, set up your categories first!

How to Create a Category
  1. Click "Categories" in the top navigation menu
  2. Click the "+ Add Category" button
  3. Enter a category name (e.g., "Groceries", "Salary", "Entertainment")
  4. Choose a color for easy identification
  5. Select the type: Expense or Income
  6. Click "Save" to create the category
Managing Categories
  • Edit: Click the edit icon next to any category to rename or change its color
  • Delete: Click the trash icon to remove a category (entries will become uncategorized)
  • View Count: Each category shows how many entries are assigned to it
Recommended Categories

For Expenses:

Groceries Rent/Mortgage Utilities Transportation Entertainment Dining Out Healthcare Shopping Subscriptions

For Income:

Salary Freelance Investments Bonus Gifts Refunds
Pro Tip

BudgetPulse's AI can automatically suggest categories based on your entry descriptions. Enable this in Settings > AI Settings!

Step 2 Adding Income & Expense Entries

Now let's add your first financial entries! You can add both income (money coming in) and expenses (money going out).

Adding an Expense
  1. Click "+ Add Entry" button (visible on Dashboard or Entries page)
  2. Select "Expense" as the entry type
  3. Enter the amount (e.g., 50.00)
  4. Select a category from your list
  5. Choose the date of the transaction
  6. Add an optional note/description
  7. Click "Save Entry"
Adding Income
  1. Click "+ Add Entry" button
  2. Select "Income" as the entry type
  3. Enter the amount received
  4. Select an income category (e.g., Salary)
  5. Choose the date and add notes
  6. Click "Save Entry"
Managing Entries
Action How To
View All Go to Entries from the menu
Edit Entry Click the pencil icon next to any entry
Quick Edit Amount Click directly on the amount to edit inline
Delete Entry Click the trash icon (cannot be undone!)
Filter Entries Use date range, category, or type filters
Sort Entries Click column headers to sort
Date Selection Tips
  • Default date is today
  • Click the calendar icon to pick any date
  • You can add entries for past dates (backdate receipts)
  • Future dates are useful for planned expenses

Step 3 Understanding Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is the command center for your finances. Here's what each section shows you.

Total Income

Shows all money received during the selected period. Includes salary, freelance work, investments, and other income sources.

Total Expenses

Shows all money spent during the selected period. Includes all your expense categories combined.

Balance

Your net position: Income minus Expenses. Green means you're saving money, red means you're spending more than earning.

Filtering Your Dashboard
  • Date Range: Click the date picker to select a custom period
    • Today, This Week, This Month
    • Last 7/30/90 Days
    • Custom date range
  • Category Filter: Focus on specific categories
  • Type Filter: Show only income or expenses
Dashboard Charts
  • Daily Chart: Line graph showing spending over time
  • Pie Chart: Visual breakdown by category
  • Bar Chart: Compare categories side by side
Hover over charts to see detailed values!

Step 4 Using the Calendar View

The calendar gives you a visual overview of your financial activity day by day.

Navigating the Calendar
  1. Go to "Calendar" from the menu
  2. Use < and > arrows to change months
  3. Click "Today" to jump back to current month
  4. Use the month/year dropdown for quick navigation
Understanding the Markers
Green Income only on this date
Red Expenses only on this date
Yellow Both income and expenses (mixed)
Bill Icon Recurring bill/subscription due
Viewing Date Details

Hover Method:

  • Hover your mouse over any date with a marker
  • A tooltip will show:
    • Total income for the day
    • Total expenses for the day
    • Net balance
    • First 3 entries with details
    • Bill/subscription information

Click Method:

  • Click any date to see all entries for that day
  • Full entry details with edit/delete options
Future Bills

The calendar shows your upcoming bills and subscriptions on future dates, so you can plan ahead for upcoming payments!

Step 6 Using AI Features

BudgetPulse uses artificial intelligence to help you manage your finances smarter.

Automatic Category Suggestions

When you add a new entry with a description, the AI can automatically suggest the best category:

  1. Enable in Settings > AI Settings
  2. When adding an entry, type a description (e.g., "Coffee at Starbucks")
  3. AI suggests "Dining Out" or "Coffee" category
  4. Accept the suggestion or choose manually
  5. AI learns from your corrections!
Smart Insights

AI analyzes your spending patterns and provides:

  • Spending Patterns: Identifies your habits
  • Saving Opportunities: Where you can cut costs
  • Budget Recommendations: Suggested budget limits
  • Anomaly Alerts: Unusual spending warnings
Predictive Analytics
  • Next Month Forecast: Predicts your spending
  • Cash Flow Projection: Future income/expense balance
  • Category Predictions: Expected spending per category
  • Budget Status: Will you stay within budget?
Anomaly Detection

AI alerts you about unusual transactions:

  • Unusually large purchases
  • Spending spikes in specific categories
  • Changed subscription amounts
  • Pattern deviations
AI Settings Location

Manage all AI features at:

Step 7 Managing Bills & Subscriptions

Never miss a payment! Track all your recurring bills and subscriptions in one place.

Adding a Recurring Payment
  1. Go to Intelligence > Bills & Subscriptions
  2. Click "+ Add Bill/Subscription"
  3. Enter payment details:
    • Name: e.g., "Netflix", "Electric Bill"
    • Amount: Monthly payment amount
    • Category: Assign to a category
    • Frequency: Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Annual
    • Due Date: Day of month/week
    • Reminder: Days before due to remind
  4. Click "Save"
Payment Reminders
  • Set reminder days (e.g., 3 days before)
  • Reminders appear on dashboard
  • Mark payments as "Paid" when done
  • Dismiss reminders you've seen
Bill Features
Feature Description
Frequency Options Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Annual
Auto Calculation Shows monthly and annual cost totals
Calendar Integration Bills appear on calendar for future dates
Payment History Track when bills were actually paid
Pause/Resume Temporarily pause inactive subscriptions
AI Subscription Detection

BudgetPulse can automatically detect recurring payments in your spending history and suggest adding them as bills!

Step 8 Setting Financial Goals

Set goals to save money, pay off debt, or limit spending in specific categories.

Creating a Goal
  1. Go to "Goals" from the menu
  2. Click "+ Create Goal"
  3. Choose goal type:
    • Savings Goal: Save a specific amount
    • Spending Limit: Cap spending in a category
    • Debt Payoff: Track debt reduction
    • Emergency Fund: Build safety net
  4. Set your target amount
  5. Choose a deadline (optional)
  6. Add a description for motivation
  7. Click "Create Goal"
Tracking Progress
  • Progress Bar: Visual progress indicator
  • Percentage Complete: How close you are
  • Milestone Celebrations: 25%, 50%, 75% achievements
  • Auto-Update: Spending goals update automatically
  • Manual Updates: Add progress for savings goals
Goal Status
  • Active Currently working on
  • Completed Goal achieved!
  • Paused Temporarily on hold
  • Failed Deadline passed

Step 9 Using Voice Commands

Add entries hands-free by speaking naturally to BudgetPulse!

Getting Started
  1. Click the purple microphone button (bottom-right corner)
  2. Or press Ctrl + Shift + V
  3. Allow microphone access when prompted
  4. Speak your command clearly
  5. Review the parsed command
  6. Click "Confirm" to save
Example Commands
Say This Result
"Add expense 50 dollars for groceries" Creates $50 grocery expense
"Add income 2000 salary" Creates $2000 income
"Spent 25 on coffee yesterday" Creates $25 expense for yesterday
"Delete last entry" Removes most recent entry
"What's my total this month?" Shows monthly summary
Supported Browsers
  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Opera
  • Safari (limited support)
  • Firefox (not supported)
Troubleshooting
  • Make sure microphone permission is granted
  • Speak clearly in a quiet environment
  • Use English for best results
  • Check the Help page for more tips
Privacy Note

Voice processing happens entirely in your browser. No audio is sent to our servers - your voice data stays private!

Step 10 Customizing Your Settings

Personalize BudgetPulse to match your preferences.

Appearance Settings

Go to Settings > Appearance

  • Theme: Switch between Dark and Light mode
  • Compact Mode: Condensed UI for more data
  • Font Size: Small, Medium, or Large text
  • Animations: Enable/disable UI animations
Currency Settings

Go to Settings > Currency

  • Choose from 19 supported currencies
  • View current exchange rates
  • All amounts convert automatically
Changing currency converts all existing entries. This cannot be undone!
AI Settings

Go to Settings > AI Settings

  • Auto-Categorization: Let AI assign categories
  • Smart Suggestions: Get intelligent recommendations
  • Spending Insights: Enable spending analysis
  • Budget Predictions: Forecast future spending
Profile Settings

Go to Settings (main page)

  • Update your name and email
  • Upload a profile picture
  • Change your password
  • View account statistics
  • Delete your account (irreversible!)
