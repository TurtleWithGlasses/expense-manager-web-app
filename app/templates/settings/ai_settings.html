Confidence Thresholds
Understanding Confidence Thresholds
Minimum Confidence Threshold

The minimum confidence level required for AI to show suggestions. Higher values mean more accurate but fewer suggestions.

  • Low (10-30%): More suggestions, some may be less accurate
  • Medium (40-70%): Balanced suggestions and accuracy
  • High (80-100%): Fewer but highly accurate suggestions
Auto-accept Threshold

The confidence level at which AI suggestions are automatically accepted without your review.

  • Low (10-50%): More auto-acceptance, higher risk of errors
  • Medium (60-80%): Balanced auto-acceptance
  • High (90-100%): Very conservative, manual review required

Learning Preferences
AI Learning & Adaptation
Learn from Feedback

When enabled, the AI learns from your corrections and feedback to improve future suggestions.

  • Remembers when you change AI suggestions
  • Adapts to your personal preferences
  • Improves accuracy over time
Retrain Frequency

How often the AI model updates itself with your latest data and feedback.

  • Daily: Most responsive, uses more resources
  • Weekly: Good balance of responsiveness and efficiency
  • Monthly: Most efficient, less frequent updates
